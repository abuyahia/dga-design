The leading variant places the expand/collapse icon before the title, aligning it with the start of the text. The expanded content is indented to align under the title text, matching the icon's width and gap.

Use the leading variant when the visual hierarchy benefits from the icon acting as a disclosure indicator at the start of the row, such as in tree views, nested lists, or content-heavy layouts.
